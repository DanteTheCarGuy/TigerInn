Columbia, Missouri

Columbia is the largest city in Mid-Missouri. It's right in the middle of the state off I-70 between St. Louis and Kansas city. The city has vibrant colorful landscape with many parks and bike trails to explore. Columbia has a large downtown better know as The District, It's filled with Shops, Brewerys, and Nightlife Entertaiment. The city is ranked as one of the places to live for is Educational Faculities, Health care, Economic Growth, and Cultural Diversity.

Columbia has a population of 126,000 people. The city is perfect for sports fans. It's home to the Mizzou Tigers and our Columbia Cougars . You can find the latest sport schedules on the top left. The Tiger Inn is a Proud sponsor for thr Cougar Athlethics program. GO COUGARS!!!

Columbia is home to the annual True/False Film Festival held every March. The True/False Film Festival is the leading Documentary Film Festival in the United States. The city prides itself on the Cultural Diversity and the celebration of Music, Film, Life, with great BBQ. So come and have some Dinner and check out a Movie at RagTag CinemaThen visit the Blue Notefor some Drinks and Dancing.

If you happen to visit Columbia during the first week of October you can enjoy delicious BBQ, Brews, Blues, and Dancing at the famous Roots & Blues BBQ Festival.
