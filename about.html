About The Tiger Inn

Ameneties

  • Free Local Calls
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Free Parking
  • Hot Breakfest
  • Two Swimming Pools
  • Fully Equipped Gym

The Inn is located two blocks across the street from Missouri University . We are also within walking distance of The District which has many Shops, Boutiques, Resturants, bars, and Clubs. It has much to offer with a great night life.

The Tiger Inn has 120 units with King and queen Beds. We accomadate Weddings, Family Reunions, Banquets, Small Office Conferences. Many of our guests are alumni of mizzou and our fans who come to see the games.

Tiger Inn is very nice and affordable. All rooms are elegantly arranged with Flat screen Tv, Free local calls, Free Wi-Fi in every room. Two pools, Three laundry rooms, and parking garage are all included for our guests.

A generous hot breakfest buffet is served from 6:00 to 9:00 A.M.weekdays and From 7:00 to 10:00 A.M. on the weekends. Tiger Inn has a special kid's pool, and children's playground, and many activities during the holidays. There is also a fully equipped guym with a capacity of holding 40 people.

Conferences for up to 300 participants can be held in the inn. Larger Conferences are possiable with prior arrangments with Missouri University.
